Video Production

This page features my video production project, including the final video and a brief reflection on the planning, filming, and editing process.

Watch on YouTube

Project Overview

In this project, I planned, recorded, and edited an instructional video using digital media tools. The process included outlining the content, drafting a storyboard, setting up recording equipment, and refining the final cut through multiple editing passes.

Reflection

Through this video production experience, I learned how to balance visual design, pacing, and clarity of explanation. It also helped me think more intentionally about how to present educational content in a concise, engaging, and accessible way for learners.
