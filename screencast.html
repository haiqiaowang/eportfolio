Reflection

Creating these screencasts helped me think carefully about pacing, clarity, and visual focus. I practiced structuring demonstrations so that viewers can follow each step without feeling overwhelmed, and I learned how to highlight important interface elements to support understanding. This experience will be valuable for my future work in teaching and designing instructional materials.
